About Me

I'm a tech and game enthusiast from southern Ontario, Canada. I graduated with a Bachelor of Computing, with Honours, from Queen's University in 2023, Specializing in Computer Science. I also worked alongside a few of my fellow graduates to create our own game development studio, GnomeFire Labs, and developed our capstone project into a fully-fledged release on Steam, RaceTrap, the following year.
